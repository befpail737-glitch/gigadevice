Our Story

From a small trading company to a global GigaDevice authorized distributor. A journey of trust, growth, and unwavering commitment to quality.

Our Journey

2010

The Beginning

Litong Group was founded in Shenzhen, China, starting as a small electronic components trading company with just 5 employees and a vision to bridge the gap between manufacturers and global markets.

2013

First Major Partnership

Established our first direct partnership with GigaDevice Semiconductor, becoming an authorized distributor for their Flash Memory products in the Asia-Pacific region.

2016

GD32 MCU Launch

When GigaDevice launched the GD32 series of ARM Cortex-M microcontrollers, we were among the first distributors to receive authorization, investing heavily in FAE training and technical support capabilities.

2018

Global Expansion

Opened our first international offices in Dallas, USA and Munich, Germany. Expanded our team to 50+ employees and established local warehousing for faster delivery.

2020

Pandemic Resilience

During the global semiconductor shortage, we leveraged our strong supplier relationships to maintain 95%+ stock availability, supporting critical industries including medical devices and automotive.

2023

Premium Distributor Status

Achieved GigaDevice Premium Distributor status, recognizing our consistent performance, technical expertise, and customer satisfaction scores above 95%.

2025

Today & Beyond

Now serving 50+ countries with 8 certified FAEs, 4 global offices, and a commitment to being the most trusted GigaDevice distributor worldwide. Our ISO 9001 certification journey is underway.
