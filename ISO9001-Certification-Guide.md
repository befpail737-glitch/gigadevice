## 1. Understanding ISO 9001:2015

### What is ISO 9001:2015?

ISO 9001:2015 is the international standard for Quality Management Systems (QMS). It provides a framework for organizations to consistently meet customer and regulatory requirements while demonstrating continuous improvement.

### Why It Matters for Litong Group

| Benefit | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| **Enhanced Credibility** | Demonstrates commitment to quality to GigaDevice and customers |
| **Market Access** | Required by many enterprise customers and government contracts |
| **Operational Efficiency** | Streamlined processes reduce errors and costs |
| **Competitive Advantage** | Differentiator in the semiconductor distribution market |
| **SEO/GEO Value** | Trust signal for AI search engines and customers |

### Key Principles

1. **Customer Focus** - Meeting customer requirements and exceeding expectations
2. **Leadership** - Establishing unity of purpose and direction
3. **Engagement of People** - Competent, empowered, and engaged personnel
4. **Process Approach** - Understanding and managing interrelated processes
5. **Improvement** - Ongoing focus on improvement
6. **Evidence-Based Decision Making** - Decisions based on data analysis
7. **Relationship Management** - Managing relationships with interested parties

## 7. Certification Audit Process

### Stage 1 Audit (Documentation Review)

**Duration:** 1-2 days  
**Focus:** Documentation completeness and readiness

**Activities:**
- Review Quality Manual and procedures
- Verify scope of certification
- Assess readiness for Stage 2
- Identify any major gaps

**Outcomes:**
- Report with findings
- List of concerns to address
- Confirmation of Stage 2 readiness

### Stage 2 Audit (Full System Audit)

**Duration:** 2-4 days (depending on scope)  
**Focus:** Implementation effectiveness

**Activities:**
- Interview personnel at all levels
- Observe processes in action
- Review records and evidence
- Verify corrective actions from Stage 1

**Possible Findings:**
- **Major Nonconformity** - Must be resolved before certification
- **Minor Nonconformity** - Must be resolved within defined timeframe
- **Opportunity for Improvement** - Recommendation only

### Certification Decision

- Certificate issued if no major nonconformities
- Minor nonconformities may require evidence of correction
- Certificate valid for 3 years
- Surveillance audits annually

## 8. Post-Certification Maintenance

### Annual Surveillance Audits

- Conducted once per year
- Focus on specific processes each year
- Verify continued compliance
- Review corrective actions

### Recertification (Every 3 Years)

- Full system audit similar to initial certification
- Opportunity to expand or reduce scope
- New 3-year certificate issued

### Continuous Improvement

**Monthly:**
- Monitor quality objectives
- Review customer feedback
- Track nonconformities

**Quarterly:**
- Department performance reviews
- Training effectiveness review
- Process performance analysis

**Annually:**
- Management review
- Internal audits
- Risk assessment update
- Quality policy review
