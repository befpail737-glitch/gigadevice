Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about GigaDevice Flash

What is the maximum erase/write cycle count?

The GD25Q series typically offers 100,000 program/erase cycles, ensuring long-term reliability for most applications.

Does GigaDevice offer automotive-grade Flash?

Yes, GigaDevice provides automotive-grade Flash memory, such as the GD25/GD55 series, designed to meet stringent automotive requirements.

What is the difference between SPI NOR and SPI NAND?

SPI NOR offers faster random access and is ideal for code execution, while SPI NAND provides higher density at lower cost, suitable for data storage.
