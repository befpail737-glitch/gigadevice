Returns & Refund Policy

Quality Guarantee | 30-Day Return Window

Our Commitment

At Litong Group, we stand behind the quality of every GigaDevice product we distribute. All products are 100% original and undergo rigorous quality control before shipment.

1. Return Eligibility

Defective Products

  • Products with manufacturing defects are eligible for return within 30 days of delivery
  • Defects must be reported within 7 days of discovery
  • Products must be in original packaging with all accessories

Incorrect Items

  • If you receive the wrong product, we will exchange it at no cost
  • Report within 14 days of delivery

Non-Returnable Items

  • Products damaged due to misuse, improper handling, or customer error
  • Products with removed or damaged serial numbers
  • Custom or special order items
  • Products returned after 30 days

2. Return Process

  1. Contact Us: Email support@litong-group.com with your order number and issue description
  2. Return Authorization: We will provide a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number within 2 business days
  3. Ship the Product: Package the product securely and ship to our local facility with the RMA number clearly marked
  4. Inspection: We will inspect the returned product within 5 business days of receipt
  5. Refund/Exchange: Approved returns will be processed for refund or exchange

3. Refund Policy

Refund Methods

  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method
  • Bank transfers: 5-10 business days
  • Credit cards: 7-14 business days (depending on your bank)

Refund Amount

  • Full refund for defective or incorrect products
  • Original shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error
  • Return shipping costs are covered by Litong Group for defective products

4. Exchange Policy

We offer exchanges for:

  • Defective products (same model replacement)
  • Incorrect items (correct model replacement)

Exchanges will be shipped after we receive and inspect the returned item.

5. Warranty Information

All GigaDevice products come with:

  • Standard Warranty: 12 months from date of purchase
  • Extended Warranty: Available for select industrial and automotive products
  • Warranty covers manufacturing defects under normal use conditions

6. Regional Policies

Korea

  • Returns accepted at Seoul facility
  • Consumer protection under Korean Fair Trade Act

Italy & Poland (EU)

  • 14-day cooling-off period for consumers (EU Consumer Rights Directive)
  • Returns accepted at Milan and Warsaw facilities

Other Countries

  • Contact local office for specific return procedures
  • International returns may require additional shipping time

Need Help?

Phone: Contact your local office

Hours: Monday - Friday, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM (Local Time)
