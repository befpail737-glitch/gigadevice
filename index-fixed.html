Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are you an authorized GigaDevice distributor?
A: Yes, Litong Group is an officially authorized distributor of GigaDevice Semiconductor. We have direct partnerships with GigaDevice and can provide authorization certificates upon request. All our products are 100% authentic and come with full manufacturer warranty.
Q: How does your pricing compare to Mouser and Digi-Key?
A: As a core distributor, we offer competitive pricing that is typically 15-30% lower than major distributors like Mouser and Digi-Key. We also offer volume discounts and flexible payment terms for qualified customers.
Q: What is your delivery time?
A: We maintain local stock in USA, Germany, India, and China. For in-stock items, we offer same-day or next-day shipping. Typical delivery times are: USA (1-3 days), Germany/EU (2-3 days), India (72 hours), Asia Pacific (3-5 days).
Q: Do you provide technical support?
A: Yes, we have a team of GigaDevice-certified FAEs (Field Application Engineers) who provide free technical consultation. We can assist with MCU selection, migration from other brands, development tool recommendations, and troubleshooting.
Q: What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
A: We have no minimum order quantity. You can order as little as 1 piece for most standard products. This makes us ideal for prototyping and small-batch production.
