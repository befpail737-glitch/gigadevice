Litong Group Inaugurates State-of-the-Art Technical Support Center for GigaDevice Products

Published on: July 1, 2025 | Category: Corporate News

[City, Country] â€?July 1, 2025 â€?Litong Group, an authorized distributor of GigaDevice Semiconductor Inc., today announced the official opening of its new, dedicated Technical Support Center. This strategic investment underscores Litong Group's unwavering commitment to providing unparalleled service and comprehensive technical assistance to customers utilizing GigaDevice's extensive product portfolio.

The new center is equipped with advanced testing facilities, a wide array of GigaDevice development kits, and a team of highly skilled Field Application Engineers (FAEs) specializing in GigaDevice microcontrollers, Flash memory, and other solutions. The FAE team will offer in-depth technical consultation, design-in support, troubleshooting, and training to help customers accelerate their product development cycles and overcome complex technical challenges.

"The launch of our new Technical Support Center marks a significant milestone in our mission to be the most reliable and value-added partner for GigaDevice customers," said [Spokesperson Name], [Spokesperson Title] at Litong Group. "We understand the critical role that robust technical support plays in today's fast-paced electronics industry. This center will serve as a hub of expertise, ensuring our customers receive timely and effective assistance, enabling them to bring innovative products to market faster."

The center's services will cover a broad spectrum of GigaDevice products, including the GD32 MCU series (ARM Cortex-M and RISC-V), SPI NOR/NAND Flash memory, and analog & power solutions. Customers can access support through various channels, including online portals, dedicated hotlines, and on-site visits for complex projects.

Litong Group invites all GigaDevice users and partners to leverage the resources and expertise available at the new Technical Support Center. This initiative is part of Litong Group's continuous effort to enhance customer satisfaction and foster long-term relationships within the semiconductor ecosystem.

Key Offerings of the New Technical Support Center:

  • **Expert FAE Team**: Highly skilled engineers specializing in GigaDevice products.
  • **Comprehensive Support**: Design-in assistance, troubleshooting, and technical consultation.
  • **Advanced Facilities**: Equipped with state-of-the-art testing and development tools.
  • **Broad Product Coverage**: Support for GD32 MCUs, Flash memory, and Analog & Power solutions.
