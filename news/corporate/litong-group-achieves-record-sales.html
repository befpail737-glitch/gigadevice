Litong Group Sets New Sales Record for GigaDevice Flash Memory Portfolio

Published on: October 15, 2025 | Category: Corporate News

[City, Country] – October 15, 2025 – Litong Group, a leading authorized distributor of GigaDevice Semiconductor Inc., proudly announced today that it has achieved record-breaking sales figures for GigaDevice's comprehensive Flash memory portfolio. This significant milestone underscores the growing market demand for GigaDevice's high-quality and reliable memory solutions, as well as Litong Group's effective distribution strategies and strong customer relationships.

The record sales performance spans across GigaDevice's popular SPI NOR Flash (GD25Q series) and SPI NAND Flash (GD5F series) products, which are widely adopted in various applications including consumer electronics, industrial control, automotive systems, and IoT devices. The robust demand reflects the industry's recognition of GigaDevice's technological advancements and product reliability.

"We are incredibly proud of our team's dedication and the strong partnership we share with GigaDevice," stated [Spokesperson Name], [Spokesperson Title] at Litong Group. "Achieving record sales for their Flash memory products is a testament to the superior quality and competitive edge that GigaDevice brings to the market. It also highlights our commitment to providing our customers with access to the best components and unparalleled support."

Litong Group attributes this success to several factors, including its extensive global sales network, proactive inventory management, and a highly skilled Field Application Engineering (FAE) team that provides crucial technical support and design-in assistance to customers. The company's ability to ensure timely delivery and offer tailored solutions has been pivotal in meeting the diverse needs of its client base.

As the demand for advanced memory solutions continues to grow, Litong Group remains committed to strengthening its collaboration with GigaDevice and expanding its offerings to support the evolving needs of the global electronics industry.

Key Factors Contributing to Record Sales:

  • **GigaDevice Product Excellence**: High quality, reliability, and competitive performance of Flash memory.
  • **Litong's Distribution Network**: Extensive global reach and efficient supply chain management.
  • **Expert Technical Support**: Dedicated FAE team providing valuable design-in and post-sales assistance.
  • **Strong Customer Relationships**: Building trust and delivering value to clients worldwide.

For more information on GigaDevice Flash memory products or to inquire about distribution services, please contact Litong Group's sales team.
