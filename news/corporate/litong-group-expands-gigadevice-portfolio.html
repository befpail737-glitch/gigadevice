Litong Group Strengthens GigaDevice Offerings with New Automotive-Grade Flash and GD32H7 MCUs

Published on: November 25, 2025 | Category: Corporate News

[City, Country] – November 25, 2025 – Litong Group, an authorized distributor of GigaDevice Semiconductor Inc., today announced a significant expansion of its GigaDevice product portfolio. The expanded offerings now include GigaDevice's latest automotive-grade Flash memory solutions and the highly anticipated GD32H7 series of high-performance microcontrollers.

The introduction of automotive-grade Flash memory addresses the growing demand for robust and reliable storage solutions in the rapidly evolving automotive electronics market. These new Flash products are designed to meet stringent automotive industry standards, offering extended temperature ranges, enhanced endurance, and superior data retention capabilities crucial for in-vehicle applications such as ADAS, infotainment systems, and body control modules.

Complementing the Flash expansion, Litong Group is also bringing the cutting-edge GD32H7 series MCUs to its customers. The GD32H7 series, based on the ARM Cortex-M7 core, represents a new benchmark in performance for GigaDevice's microcontroller lineup. With clock speeds exceeding 600MHz, advanced DSP capabilities, and rich connectivity options, the GD32H7 MCUs are ideally suited for high-end industrial control, artificial intelligence, advanced motor drives, and complex human-machine interface applications.

"We are thrilled to enhance our GigaDevice portfolio with these critical new products," said [Spokesperson Name], [Spokesperson Title] at Litong Group. "The automotive-grade Flash and GD32H7 series MCUs empower our customers to develop next-generation products that demand the highest levels of performance, reliability, and safety. This expansion reinforces our commitment to providing comprehensive and leading-edge solutions to the market."

Litong Group will provide full technical support, including design-in assistance, sample availability, and competitive pricing for the new GigaDevice products. Customers can contact Litong Group's sales and FAE teams for more information and to discuss their specific project requirements.

Key Highlights of the Expanded Portfolio:

  • **Automotive-Grade Flash Memory**: Designed for extreme conditions, high reliability, and long-term data integrity in automotive applications.
  • **GD32H7 Series MCUs**: Ultra-high performance with ARM Cortex-M7 core, advanced DSP, and extensive peripherals for demanding applications.
  • **Enhanced Support**: Litong Group's expert FAE team offers comprehensive technical assistance for seamless integration.

For more information on GigaDevice's automotive-grade Flash memory and GD32H7 series MCUs, please visit Litong Group's product pages or contact our sales team.
