Automotive Central Control & Dashboard

Robust and safe components for advanced in-vehicle systems.

Home > Solutions > Automotive Central Control & Dashboard

Solution Summary

This GigaDevice-based solution provides a highly reliable and safe platform for automotive central control units and digital dashboards. Leveraging automotive-grade GD25/GD55 Flash memory and GD32A503 series MCUs, it delivers robust performance, enhanced security, and compliance with automotive industry standards. The solution is designed to meet the stringent requirements of in-vehicle systems, ensuring stable operation, fast boot-up, and secure data handling for critical automotive applications.

Solution Block Diagram

Conceptual block diagram illustrating the key components of the automotive central control and dashboard solution.

Core Advantages

  • **Automotive-Grade Components**: GD25/GD55 Flash and GD32A503 MCUs meet AEC-Q100 standards.
  • **High Reliability**: Designed for harsh automotive environments with extended temperature ranges and robust ESD protection.
  • **Enhanced Security**: Hardware-level security features for data protection and anti-tampering.
  • **Fast Boot-up**: Optimized Flash memory and MCU performance for quick system initialization.
  • **Rich Connectivity**: Integrated interfaces for CAN, LIN, Ethernet, and other automotive protocols.
