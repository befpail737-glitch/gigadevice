Smart Metering & Power Collection

Reliable and secure solutions for energy management.

Home > Solutions > Smart Metering & Power Collection

Solution Summary

This GigaDevice-based solution offers a robust and secure platform for smart metering and power collection applications. Leveraging the ultra-low-power GD32L series MCUs and high-reliability SPI Flash memory, it provides accurate measurement, secure data storage, and efficient communication capabilities. The solution is designed to meet the stringent requirements of energy management systems, ensuring long battery life, data integrity, and compliance with industry standards.

Solution Block Diagram

Conceptual block diagram illustrating the key components of the smart metering and power collection solution.

Core Advantages

  • **Ultra-Low-Power MCU**: GD32L series for extended battery life in smart meters.
  • **Secure Data Storage**: High-reliability SPI Flash for critical metering data.
  • **Accurate Measurement**: High-precision ADCs and integrated peripherals for precise data acquisition.
  • **Robust Communication**: Support for various communication protocols (e.g., PLC, RF, NB-IoT) for data transmission.
  • **Enhanced Security**: Hardware-level security features for data protection and anti-tampering.
